The Ethics of ChatGPT

December 10, 2022

ChatGPT is a seemingly revolutionary new tool that allows one to do just about everything. But, is it too powerful? Or we just being tricked to believe that it is? From highly practical applications to ethically questionable ones, especially regarding students, tools like this are sure to have a profound impact on tech and society in general.

Tech Layoffs and the Economy

December 1, 2022

The last two years, and more broadly, the last decade, was filled with a booming tech industry and promises of a good life achieved through studying computer science and becoming a software engineer. Hoards of students picked up CS with these stories in mind, but the landscape has changed significantly. The classic tech success story is slowly becoming closer to a dream than the reality it once was. Is this really the end of booming tech or can we hope for better times ahead?
